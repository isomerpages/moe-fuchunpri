<h4>North West Outstanding School Partner Award</h4>
<p>The North West Outstanding School Partner Award is a recognition given
by Singapore’s North West Community Development Council to schools that
actively partner the community through outreach, volunteering, and environmental
or social initiatives.</p>
<p>On 23 May 2026, Fuchun Primary School was presented the Silver Award by
the Mayor of North West CDC Mr Alex Yam, for its sustained collaboration
with NWCDC.</p>
