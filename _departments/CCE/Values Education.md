<h4><strong>Values Education (Fuchun Champions)</strong></h4>
<p>Through our Values Education Programme, we hope to impart all students
our school core values of Passion for Learning, Integrity, Empathy, Respect
and Resilience. These 5 values are represented by our 5 Fuchun Champions,
superheroes whom students can look up to, aspiring to be like them while
having fun in the process!</p>
