<p>In 2025, we launched our <strong>Interdisciplinary Project Work (IPW)</strong>,
and our students embarked on meaningful projects focused on <strong>sustainability</strong>.</p>
<p>Tasked with designing innovative solutions to reduce waste in school,
students applied their learning across subjects to solve real-world problems.
Through this project, they developed <strong>adaptive thinking</strong> and
honed their <strong>collaborative problem-solving skills</strong> working
with their classmates. The experience also reinforced key <strong>21st Century Competencies (21CC)</strong> such
as <strong>curiosity</strong> and <strong>empathy</strong>, empowering them
to make a positive impact within their school community.</p>
<p>More importantly, they had fun working together with their friends on
the projects too!</p>
